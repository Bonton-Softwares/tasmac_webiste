TASMAC POLICY

Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are important to us.

Introduction

The TASMAC Portal respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. It does not automatically collect any personal details, such as your name, phone number, or email address, that could be used to identify you individually.

If the Portal requires you to provide personal information, you will be informed about the specific purpose of collecting this data. Adequate security measures will always be in place to ensure the protection of your personal information.

Data Protection and Usage

The TASMAC Portal ensures that any personally identifiable information voluntarily provided by users is not sold or shared with any third party, whether public or private.

All information shared with the Portal is safeguarded against risks such as loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

TASMAC reserves the right to modify, amend, or remove this privacy statement at any time without prior notice.

This policy does not establish any form of contract or agreement between TASMAC and users visiting the Portal or sharing identifying information.
